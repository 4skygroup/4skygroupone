News, Press Release 03 December 2025

4 Sky Group - Acquisition of PBM Company

4 Sky Group is proud to officially announce the successful acquisition of PBM, a historically renowned company specializing in high-grade metallurgy and heavy industrial components. This milestone marks a significant expansion of our operational footprint across Europe.

This strategic acquisition will be directly integrated into our LineSky 360 division, which is dedicated to the challenges of the industrial, advanced hardware technology, and global infrastructure sectors.

Strengthening Our Industrial Core

By bringing PBM into the 4 Sky Group ecosystem, we aim to modernize traditional metallurgical processes through our expertise in 360° Project Management and AI integration. PBM's exceptional heritage combined with our forward-thinking strategy will allow us to optimize value chains and accelerate the energy transition within heavy industries.

Robbin James AGOH, CEO of 4 Sky Group, stated: "The integration of PBM is not just an acquisition; it is a convergence of traditional industrial excellence and modern strategic management. We are ready to steer PBM toward a new era of global competitiveness."
