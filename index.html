Our Brands

Rjayna embodies our exclusive center of expertise reserved for the world of luxury and high-end hospitality. We support prestigious houses, designer brands, and exceptional establishments in their development and pursuit of excellence. Through bespoke support in project management and strategy consulting, Rjayna helps luxury leaders elevate the customer experience, innovate while honoring their heritage, and consolidate their positioning on the global stage.

A premier strategy consulting and project management firm, McKinSky 360 supports decision-makers in the transformation, growth, and optimization of their organizations. We combine a 360-degree vision with proven methodologies to solve the most complex challenges across the tertiary, institutional, and fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) sectors.

LineSky 360 is our expert branch dedicated to the challenges of the industrial, advanced hardware technology, and global infrastructure sectors. We steer large-scale projects and design robust strategies to optimize value chains, accelerate the energy transition, and drive innovation at the heart of heavy industries.

Work with 4 Sky Group

Working at 4 Sky Group means engaging in a passionate profession with multiple facets, contributing to major projects for leading companies and institutions. It involves collaborating with ambitious leaders to structure, manage, and accelerate significant transformations — from strategic consulting to operational project management. The core of the learning experience happens at the heart of missions, through direct client interaction and alongside demanding multidisciplinary teams. At 4 Sky Group, everyone has the opportunity to build their own career path according to their ambitions: mobility between subsidiaries, sectoral specialization, and accelerated responsibility acquisition. Excellence, team spirit, and commitment are at the foundation of our culture.
